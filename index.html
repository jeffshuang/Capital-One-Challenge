Map of Most Popular Listings

Heat map of listings that are two standard deviations above the average number of reviews.

Map of Most Expensive Listings

Heat map of listings that are two standard deviations above the average nightly price of the listings

Price was determined by nightly price of the listing due to having the most data points. Average price of listing was found to be $250.16 per night.

Map of Highest Reviewed Listings

Heat map of all listings with a perfect review score.

Uses the overall average review score rather than the the sub-review scores.

Most Popular Neighborhood:

Mission District

Based on the data and map visualziataion, Mission District both has the most reviews and the most highly rated reviews of all neighborhoods.

Where to Invest:

Mission District

Based on the data and map visualziataion, Mission District would provide the best place to invest $100 million given that it is the region both with most popularity, highest rated reviews, and the region with the highest average cost.
